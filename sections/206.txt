Sec. TPR-206. Auburndale Village Parking District 

(a) There shall be a neighborhood parking district established known as the Auburndale village parking district, 
which shall be operated in accordance with the terms of this section pursuant to Chapter 19 Section 202 of the 
Revised Ordinances of Newton, Massachusetts, 2012, as amended. 

(b) A vehicle properly displaying an Auburndale or Auburndale business parking permit issued in accordance with 

this section shall be exempt from the parking restrictions set out in section TPR-176. 

(c) An Auburndale or Auburndale business parking permit will be valid on the following streets or sections of 

streets, except where otherwise noted:

Bourne Street

Johnson Place

(d) The public streets and private ways listed in Section TPR-206(c) shall have one of the following restrictions in 
place: the following: 

(1) No parking any time. 

(2) No Parking Monday to Friday. 

(3) No Parking Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. 

(4) 15-Minute limit. 

(5) One hour limit. 

(6) Two hour limit. 

(7) Two hour limit, all days, all times. 

(8) Parking by Auburndale Permit only, all days, all times. 

(9) One hour limit, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., except by Auburndale permit. 

(10) Two hour limit, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., except by Auburndale permit. 

(11) Parking by Auburndale Business Permit only, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. 

(12) Two hour metered parking. 

(13) Twelve hour metered parking. 

(14) No parking, 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. and 4:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., all days. 

(15) School Blue zone. 

(16) No parking, 8:00am to 10:00 am except Saturdays, Sundays and Holidays as well as resident parking only, 

Monday to Friday, September 1 to May 15. 

(17) One-hour limit, all days, all times, except by Auburndale Permit. 

(18) One hour limit, September 1 to May 15, except holidays or by Auburndale Permit. 

(19) Parking by Auburndale Permit only, Monday to Friday, September 1 to May 15. 

(e) The maximum number of Auburndale parking permits issued to a resident shall be limited to two (2) per dwelling 

unit. 

(f) Residents living in housing units on streets or street segments included in section TPR-206(c) which do not allow 
for parking at any time, or which are on average less than 20 feet in width, may obtain Auburndale parking 
permits and visitor passes as provided under this section for another street listed in Section TPR-206(c). 

(g) The maximum number of Auburndale parking permits issued to a non-resident shall be as follows: 

(1) Up to twenty-four (24) Auburndale business permits for Auburn Street. 

(2) Up to fifty-five (55) Auburndale business permits for Central Street. 

(3) Up to twenty five (25) Auburndale parking permits for Hancock Street. Up to fifteen of these permits may 
be issued to the Williams School for distribution to staff and up to ten of these permits may be issued to the 
Williams School aftercare program staff shall be considered visitor permits according to section 19-202. 

(4) Up to ten (10) Auburndale parking permits for Lasell Street shall be issued to the Williams School for 

distribution to staff and shall be considered visitor permits according to section 19-202. 

(5) Up to six (6) Auburndale business permits for Melrose Avenue. 

(6) Up to four (4) Auburndale parking permits for and five (5) Auburndale business permits for Melrose Street. 

(7) Up to ten (10) Auburndale business permits for Woodbine Street. 

(h) Resident and non-resident permits are not available to persons living in dormitories. 

(i) Regardless of street address or driveway location, residents living in housing units on streets or street segments 
included in section TPR-206(c) which abut more than one street included in section TPR-206(c) and which allow 
parking by Auburndale parking permit may choose which of the abutting streets upon which the Auburndale 
resident parking permits and/or visitor parking permits shall be valid.
