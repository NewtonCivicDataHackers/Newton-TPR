(a) There shall be a neighborhood parking district established in accordance with the Newton revised ordinance 
section 19-202 known as the Newton Highlands parking district, which shall be operated in accordance with 
the terms of this section.  

(b) A vehicle properly displaying a Newton Highlands parking district permit issued in accordance with this section 

shall be exempt from the parking restrictions set out in section TPR-176.  

(c) The following streets or sections of streets compose the geographic boundary of the Newton Highlands parking 

district:  

Bowdoin Street (Erie Street to Lincoln Street) 

Chester Street (Forest to Lincoln) 

Columbus Street (Forest Street to Lincoln Street) 

Erie Avenue 

Hartford Street 

Lincoln Street 

Standish Street 

Woodward Street (Lincoln Street to Boylston Street, including 991-1005 Boylston Street) 

(d) The public streets and private ways listed in Section TPR-209(c) shall have one of the following restrictions in 

place: 

(1) No parking any time. 

(2) One-hour limit, 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., including Saturdays, except by Newton Highlands Permit. 

(3) Two-hour limit, 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m., including Saturdays, except by Newton Highlands Permit. 

(4) Loading Zone, 1:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. 

(5) Two-hour metered parking zone. 

(e) The maximum number of Newton Highlands parking district permits issued to a resident shall be limited to two 

(2) per dwelling unit. 

(f) Residents living in housing units on streets or street segments included in section TPR-209(c) which do not allow 
for parking at any time, or which are on average less than 20 feet in width, may obtain Newton Highlands 
parking district permits and visitor permits as provided under this section for another street listed in Section 
TPR-209(c). 

(g) The maximum number of Newton Highlands parking district permits issued to a non-resident shall be as follows: 

(1) Up to one (1) on Columbus Street. 

(2) Up to one (1) on Bowdoin Street.
