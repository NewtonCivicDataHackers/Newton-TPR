Sec. TPR-176. Parking regulations pertaining to particular streets.* 

  (a) The following rules shall apply in the construction of this section: 

(1) Provisions of this section prohibiting parking shall be in effect at all times except Saturdays, Sundays and legal 

holidays unless otherwise noted. 

(2) Provisions of this section limiting the period which a vehicle may be parked shall be in effect on all days except 

Saturdays, Sundays and legal holidays unless otherwise noted. 

(3)  Angle parking regulations shall be in effect at all times unless otherwise noted. 

(4) Parking meter zone regulations shall be in effect at all times except Sundays and legal holidays unless otherwise 

noted. 

(5)  Provisions  of  this  section  designated  “School  Days”  shall  be  in  effect  Monday  through  Friday  when  the 

Newton Public Schools are in session during the regular academic year. 

  (b) Parking shall be prohibited at locations designated by the commissioner of public works in accordance with this 
subsection.
