Sec. TPR-201. Overnight on-street angle parking areas. 

The following on-street angle parking areas are hereby designated for resident permit overnight parking as described in 
Sec. 19-175(c) of Newton Revised Ordinances, 2012. 

  Chestnut Street:  between Oak Street and Linden Street, up to 25 spaces. 

(TPR-265, 11-17-16)
