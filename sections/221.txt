Sec. TPR-221. Signs to be erected. 

Temporary signs bearing the scheduled time of game commencement shall be placed at the following locations on 
the day that football game day parking restrictions are in effect: 

  Beacon Street, at the Boston line. 

  Beacon Street, at Centre Street. 

  Centre Street, at Beacon Street. 

  Centre Street, at Cabot Street. 

  Commonwealth Avenue, at the Boston line. 

  Commonwealth Avenue, at Centre Street. 

  Hammond Pond Parkway, at Beacon Street. 

  Hammond Street, at the Brookline line Waverley Avenue, at Kenrick Street. 

(Ord. No. Z-19, 03-17-08)
