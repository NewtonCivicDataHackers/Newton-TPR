Sec. TPR-83. Use and operation of heavy commercial vehicles restricted on certain streets. 

  (a) The use or operation of heavy commercial vehicles as defined in Sec. 19-1 of the Newton ordinances, is hereby 
prohibited upon those ways or portions thereof which are set forth in this section, except when such vehicles, for the 
purpose of delivering or picking up goods, wares, merchandise or materials are traveling to or from destinations on 
such ways, or when such vehicles are traveling for such purposes to or from abutting lands or buildings or adjoining 
ways or streets to which access cannot otherwise be gained. 

Albemarle Road. Commercial motor vehicles in excess of five (5) tons carrying capacity between North Street 

and Crafts Street from 10:00 p.m. to 8:00 a.m. 

Albemarle Road. Commercial motor vehicles in excess of five (5) tons carrying capacity between Watertown 
Street and Crafts Street. The alternative route shall be via Watertown Street easterly to Walnut Street, and via 
Walnut Street northerly to Crafts Street.

  (b) This section shall not apply to heavy commercial vehicles going to or coming from places upon said streets for 
the purpose of making deliveries of goods, materials or merchandise to or similar collections from abutting land or 
buildings or adjoining streets or ways to which access cannot otherwise be gained; or to vehicles used in connection 
with the construction, maintenance and repair of said streets or public utilities therein; or to vehicles owned by federal, 
state, municipal or public service corporations.
