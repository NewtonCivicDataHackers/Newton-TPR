Sec. TPR-207. Horace Mann Neighborhood Parking District. 

(a) There shall be a neighborhood parking district established in accordance with the Newton revised ordinance 

section 19-202 known as the Horace Mann Neighborhood parking district, which shall be operated in accordance 
with the terms of this section. 

(b) A vehicle properly displaying a Horace Mann Neighborhood parking permit issued in accordance with this section 

shall be exempt from the parking restrictions set out in section TPR-176. 

(c) A Horace Mann Neighborhood parking permit will be valid on the following sections of streets: Nevada Street

(California Street to Churchill Street) Linwood Avenue (Crafts Street to Melville Avenue). 

(d) The public streets listed in Section TPR-207(c) shall have the following restrictions in place: (1) No parking any 

time. (2) Parking by Horace Mann Neighborhood Permit only, 8:00 a.m.to 3:30 p.m., school days. (3) 2 hour limit 
except by Horace Mann Neighborhood Permit, 8:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., school days. (4) Blue Zone. 

(e) Residents living in dwelling units which abut the street segments described in Section TPR 207(c) shall be entitled 

to obtain a Horace Mann Neighborhood parking permit. The maximum number of Horace Mann Neighborhood 
parking permits issued to residents shall be limited to two (2) per dwelling unit. In addition, up to two visitor 
permits per household may be issued. 

(f) The maximum number of Horace Mann Neighborhood parking permits issued to non-residents shall be as follows: 

(1) Up to thirty-five (35). Up to twenty-two (22) of these permits may be issued to the Horace Mann School for 
distribution to staff and shall be considered visitor permits according to section 19-202. 

(g) Regardless of street address or driveway location, residents living in housing units on streets or street segments 

included in section TPR-207(c) which abut more than one street included in section TPR-207(c) and which allow 
parking by Horace Mann Neighborhood parking permit may choose which of the abutting streets upon which the 
Horace Mann Neighborhood resident parking permits and/or visitor parking permits shall be valid.
