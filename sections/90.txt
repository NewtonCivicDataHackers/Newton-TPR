Sec. TPR-90. Vehicle Restrictions on Public Ways., the following prohibitions apply: 

  Blithedale Street: No vehicle of any description, except those of resident and permit holders and service vehicles, 

shall go upon Blithedale Street between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. school days. 

  Kimball Terrace: No vehicle of any description, except those of resident and permit holders and service vehicles, 
shall go upon Kimball Terrace between 8:00 a.m. and 5:00 p.m. when public schools of the city are in session. 

  Lasell Street: No vehicle of any description, except those of resident and permit holders and service vehicles, shall 

go upon Lasell Street between 7:00 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. when public schools of the city are in session.  

  Minot Place: No vehicle of any description, except those of property owners whose access is Minot Place, and service 
vehicles, shall go upon Minot Place between 7:45 a.m. and 4:00 p.m. on any day when the public schools of the 
city are in session.  

Norwood  Avenue:  No  vehicle  of  any  description,  except  those  of  property  owners  whose  properties  abut 
Norwood Avenue or Parkview Avenue, shall enter Norwood Avenue in the easterly direction, to the east of 
Bridges Avenue.
